Your week in Training!

Great job on completing your 2 mile run. Also, we had our first Shoe clinic, and many of you have got your first pair. Congratulations!

Now that you have your running shoes, you can rest assured that you've set yourself up for safer, snugger runs going forward. After your first lace-up, take time to walk around the house so your feet and shoes can get to know each other a bit. Your shoes can feel a bit stiff and foreign during your first few runs, and that's okay. For those of you who are still looking to get running shoes, please attend an upcoming shoe clinic.

Training starts Feb 18th!!

Congratulations on taking the first giant step towards achieving your dream of running/walking a Half Marathon! Our team of experienced coaches, supportive mentors, and dedicated volunteers are here to support you through your injury-free, fun-filled journey to the finish line ...and beyond.

Here are some important things to help make your experience a pleasant one. Please read through this email completely and save it for future reference.

Communication:

Email:
Please add striders@vibha.org to your address book (to prevent getting marked as spam) and regularly check the email account that you have registered with us. Email will be our primary mode of communication. If you want to update your registered email address, please send an email to us at Info@striders.vibha.org.

Calendar:
We use Google calendar for scheduling all events. Starting the week of Feb 18, 2023, you will receive details (location/day/ time) of our weekend training sessions via a Google calendar event. If there are special events (guest speakers/ shoe clinic/ nutrition talk, etc), you will hear about this via email and calendar invite.

Mobile App:
Use the Vibha Striders Mobile (this app) to stay up to date on schedules, news and easy access to our training videos and more.

Training:

Vibha Striders 2023 is a 15 week Half Marathon Training Program, starting Feb 18, 2023, and includes:

  • Strength training (via Zoom on Mon & Wed, at 6:00 am)
  • Local mentor-led small group runs on weekdays (Tue, Thu, Fri - time and location will be communicated by your local mentor)
  • Weekend full-team group runs at various locations (8:00 am)
  • Fundamentals or Education Series (generally right after the weekend group runs)

Strength Training:

These will be held via Zoom each week: Monday & Wednesday at 6:00 am. Coaches will lead this class, show you the workout, and give you guidance on correct form. You only need a yoga mat and a foam roller for these classes (no weights or other equipment required). If you don’t already have a foam roller, either 12-inch or longer is highly recommended: https://smile.amazon.com/dp/B00XM2N06O/

Speed Coaching:

After a very successful Speed Coaching program last season, trainees can expect to improve their ease and efficiency of running, an increase in cardiovascular fitness and confidence, and a possible improvement in performance. Speed Coaching will be available only to qualified trainees (regular runners and walkers) at the discretion of speed coaches. These workouts will happen once a week - time, location and qualification process details to follow soon.

Coaches:

Our coaches are here to guide you at every step of this journey and they will be available in person at the weekend training sessions.

Mentors:

Our mentors will be there with you for every weekday run. Mentor runs are on Tuesday, Thursday, and Friday. Locations and timings will be confirmed by the Mentors on the WhatsApp groups. You can also do the prescribed weekday training by yourself or with another trainee whose schedule and location aligns with yours. Please join one or more Mentor Whatsapp groups listed above.

Fundraising:

You are required to raise a minimum of $750 for this year’s training program. You will hear from the fundraising team soon at the weekend training sessions. They will initiate your fundraising campaign and guide you through the fundraising process. If you have any fundraising questions, please send those to fundraising@striders.vibha.org.

Information Confidentiality:

All information, advice, guidance, and media given as part of the program, is for your exclusive consumption. Please do not share or distribute it further.

Questions:

Please send an email to run@striders.vibha.org with all your training related questions / concerns / comments.
If you have any ideas, suggestions, general questions, or referrals, then please send those to info@striders.vibha.org.

Training Program Referrals:

Most of our trainees join by referral. If you want your friends to join the program, please ask them to sign up on our website at http://striders.vibha.org .
